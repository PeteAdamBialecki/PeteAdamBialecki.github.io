Zombie Apocalypse Adventure Game (version 1)

Let's create a text adventure game with JavaScript. Let's set a scenario in which we encounter a zombie, choose a weapon to fight the zombie with, and have a 50/50 chance of either defeating the zombie with our weapon, or getting bitten and losing the game.

To continue building on this game, build on this starter zombie adventure game. Maybe you want to customize the ending based on the weapon choice, or continue the story. Choose from the following to build your game:

  • Strings
  • Numbers
  • Variables
  • If/Else
  • Math.round()
  • Math.random()
  • prompt()
  • alert()
  • confirm()
  • Operators like ===, !=, &&, >, <, +, -, *, /
